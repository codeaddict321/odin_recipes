Biryani

Description

Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic rice dish made with basmati rice, spices, and usually with meat, chicken, or vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice
  • Meat or vegetables
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato
  • Yogurt
  • Spices and herbs
  • Ghee or oil
  • Nuts and raisins (optional)

Steps

  1. Marinate the meat or vegetables.
  2. Prepare the rice.
  3. Saute onions, garlic, and spices.
  4. Layer rice and meat/vegetable mixture in a pot.
  5. Cover and cook on low heat until done.
  6. Garnish with nuts and raisins before serving.
