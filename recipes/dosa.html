Dosa

Description

Dosa is a popular South Indian crepe made from fermented rice and lentil batter. It is typically served with chutney and sambar.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Urad dal (black gram lentils)
  • Fenugreek seeds
  • Salt
  • Oil or ghee
  • Chutney (coconut, tomato, etc.)
  • Sambar (optional)

Steps

  1. Soak rice, dal, and fenugreek seeds overnight.
  2. Grind into a smooth batter.
  3. Ferment the batter for 6-8 hours.
  4. Heat a griddle and pour a ladleful of batter.
  5. Spread into a thin circle and drizzle with oil/ghee.
  6. Cook until golden brown on both sides.
  7. Serve hot with chutney and sambar.
